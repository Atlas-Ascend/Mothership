# ASS-LIGHT Google Play Data Safety Draft

ASS-LIGHT RC1 collects no user data and shares no user data.

- No account or identity data
- No location
- No contacts
- No microphone or audio capture
- No photos or video capture
- No browsing or search history
- No device identifiers
- No analytics
- No advertising
- No network permission

Camera permission exists only for user-commanded flashlight access through Android's torch API. Notification permission supports the visible foreground-session control. This draft must be rechecked against the final production bundle and Play Console questionnaire before submission.
