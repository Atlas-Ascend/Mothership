# ASS-LIGHT Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 12, 2026

ASS-LIGHT RC1 does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. It contains no account system, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, network permission, location access, microphone access, contact access, or remote-control feature.

The Android camera permission is requested only when the user deliberately enables the flashlight. The app uses Android's torch API and does not capture, save, or transmit photographs or video.

Notification permission is used to keep an active randomized sound session visible and stoppable. Wake-lock permission is used only during a user-started foreground session and is released by Portal Seal, service shutdown, or the six-hour safety limit.

All sounds and settings remain on the device. Uninstalling ASS-LIGHT removes its local preferences.
