# ASS-LIGHT Privacy Policy

ASS-LIGHT does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. It contains no account system, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, internet permission, location access, microphone access, contact access, or remote-control feature.

The app stores only local preferences such as whether the portal is active. These preferences remain on the device. Android camera permission is requested only to operate the phone's rear flashlight. ASS-LIGHT does not capture photographs or video.

An active randomized session uses a visible Android foreground-service notification with a stop action. The user starts every session and can end it from the app or notification.
